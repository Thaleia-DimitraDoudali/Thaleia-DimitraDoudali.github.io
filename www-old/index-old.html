About

I'm a 2nd year PhD student at School of Computer Science in College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Ada Gavrilovska.

Prior to Georgia Tech, I received my undegraduate diploma at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at National Technical University of Athens in Greece.

Research Interests

I'm currently working on managing performance of latency sensitive data serving applications on next generation Data Center platforms. More specifically, I'm looking at the response time variability of the in-memory data stores Redis and Memcached on next generation memory systems.


I'm generally interested in operating systems, next generation memory systems, large scale data management and storage, databases.

Research
