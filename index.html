About Me

I am a tenure-track faculty (Assistant Professor) at IMDEA Software Institute in Madrid, Spain.



I am looking to hire PhD students and interns! Apply here.
Come work with me on the exciting and multi-disciplinary research area of Systems, Machine Learning and Computer Vision.



I earned my PhD from the School of Computer Science in College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology (GT SCS), advised by Ada Gavrilovska.


At Georgia Tech, my research introduced system-level support for the resource management of extremely heterogeneous hardware. In particular, my dissertation adds machine intelligence to hybrid memory management.


Prior to Georgia Tech, I received an undegraduate Diploma on ECE, with major in Computer Science, at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at National Technical University of Athens in Greece.


This graduation profile featured in GT Computing news nicely summarizes my academic journey.


I got selected as a 2020 Rising Star in EECS! [GT News]
